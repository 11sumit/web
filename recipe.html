Tasty Momos Recipe

Ingredients

Here are the essential ingredients for making delecious momos:

  • Dough:2 Cups All purpose Flour
  • Water (as needed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Fillings: 500g Chicken or cabbage
  • 1 Small Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves,Minced
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger paste
  • 1 Green chilli finely Chopped
  • salt and pepper and timur
  • 1 tablespoon soya sauce

Directions

  1. Prepare the dough: In a bowl,mix the flour with salt.Gradually add water and knead until you form a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the fillings ; In another bowl,combine the chicken or banda,chopped onion,garlic,ginger,and green chilli.Season with salt,pepper and soya sauce according to your taste.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls.Roll each ball into a thin circular disc.
  4. Place a spoonfull filling in the centre of each disc.
  5. Fold edges and press to steal proprely.
  6. Steam the momos in steamer for around 15-30 minutes.
